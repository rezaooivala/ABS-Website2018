LAB TAKE OVER

Utilizing the layering and synchronization of the content media player, we treat the entire room of screens as our canvas, producing a singular piece of creative that takes over the entire space. A takeover like this creates a moment of pause, or a moment of unified branding or messaging that can be triggered to play back at regular intervals. It was a great working with David Dodge as an art director on this real collaborative project.
